**PaperclipOffice** is a _2D Pixel-Art Electron Desktop Application_ built with **TypeScript** and **React**, designed to visualize a Paperclip AI company as a living, interactive office building.

Instead of traditional dashboards, the system turns real-time data from a [Paperclip](https://github.com/paperclipai/paperclip) instance—such as agents, projects, issues, costs, and approvals—into animated entities that move, interact, and evolve across a multi-floor pixel-art environment. Each floor represents different layers of activity, creating a continuous, game-like simulation of organizational flow.

Alongside the visual simulation, **React-based panels** provide structured insights including metrics, live activity streams, and drill-down modals. This hybrid approach allows users to both _see_ the system behavior intuitively and _inspect_ detailed operational data when needed.
