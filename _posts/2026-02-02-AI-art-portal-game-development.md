**AI Art Portal for Game Development** is a _Vibe Coding Web Application_ built with **TypeScript** on the frontend and a **Python backend**, designed to streamline and scale AI-driven content creation for game development.

The platform integrates advanced models and tools such as **Nano Banana**, **Qwen Editing**, and **Wan 2.6**, enabling users to generate and refine both **images and videos** through standardized, reusable workflows. Rather than isolated prompts, it focuses on building structured pipelines that improve consistency, speed, and iteration across asset production.

At its core, the system acts as a centralized hub for managing AI workflows—allowing teams to organize, reuse, and evolve creative processes efficiently. It is particularly suited for rapid prototyping, asset generation, and experimentation in game pipelines.

Additionally, the platform supports **MCP (Model Context Protocol)**, enabling seamless integration with tools like **Claude** and **Cursor**. This allows developers and artists to automate tasks, chain workflows, and scale production using familiar AI-assisted environments.
