**Game Prototype Generation Application** is a _Vibe Coding Web Application_ built with **TypeScript** on the frontend and a **Python backend**, designed to accelerate game development through AI-powered asset creation and gameplay generation.

The system integrates **Segment Anything** to quickly extract, isolate, and reconstruct game assets from visual inputs, making it easy to reuse and adapt existing designs. This significantly reduces the time required for asset preparation and enables fast iteration across different game ideas.

On top of that, the platform leverages **Google Gemini** to generate **game logic, mechanics, and content directly from prompts**. Users can go from a simple idea to a playable prototype by combining generated assets with AI-created gameplay systems.

By bridging automated asset processing with prompt-based game generation, the project creates a streamlined workflow for **rapid prototyping**, experimentation, and concept validation—ideal for developers exploring new gameplay ideas at high speed.
