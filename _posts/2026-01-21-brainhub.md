**BrainHub** is an _AI-powered marketing platform_ built to help Vietnamese users create, optimize, and scale their marketing efforts through accessible and practical AI tools.

The platform provides a suite of **free AI tools** designed for real-world use cases—ranging from content creation and optimization to campaign ideation and automation. Instead of focusing purely on technology, BrainHub emphasizes usability and real impact for marketers, creators, and small businesses.

Beyond tools, BrainHub also serves as a **knowledge hub**, offering educational resources and a blog system written in **Markdown**. These materials are designed to guide users in understanding and applying AI effectively in everyday marketing workflows, bridging the gap between theory and practice.

[BrainHub](https://brainhub.vn/)
