BrainHub is an AI-powered marketing platform that provides free AI tools to help users (Vietnamese users) create, optimize, and scale their marketing efforts. In addition to its toolset, it offers educational resources and a knowledge-sharing blog, published in Markdown format, to guide users in effectively leveraging AI in real-world marketing scenarios.
